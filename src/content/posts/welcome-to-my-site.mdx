Welcome! If you've found your way here, you're now in my little corner of the internet where I share thoughts, experiences, and insights from both my professional and personal journey.

## What This Site Is About

This isn't just another tech blog or personal portfolio (though it functions as both). Think of it as a digital reflection of who I am—someone who spends their days building data systems and their evenings discovering new music, training for the next run, or perfecting the art of pour-over coffee.

### The Professional Side

In my day job, I'm a data engineer and software engineer who gets genuinely excited about:

- **Solving complex data problems** at scale
- **Building systems** that turn chaos into clarity
- **Leading technical teams** through challenging migrations
- **Bridging the gap** between raw data and business impact

You'll find posts here about data architecture, system design, lessons learned from production incidents, and thoughts on technical leadership. I believe in sharing knowledge openly—we all learn faster when we learn together.

### The Personal Side

But I'm more than just someone who writes code and designs pipelines. I'm also:

- A **music discovery enthusiast** always hunting for that perfect track you've never heard
- A **trail runner** who believes the best debugging happens around mile 5
- A **coffee nerd** with strong opinions about extraction ratios and grind consistency
- A **photographer** who still shoots film because some things shouldn't be rushed

## Why I Built This

After years of consuming content from other engineers and creators, I wanted to give back to the community that has taught me so much. But I also wanted to create something that reflects the full spectrum of who I am.

Too often, professional sites feel sterile—like LinkedIn profiles expanded into full websites. Personal blogs can feel too scattered. I wanted something that honored both sides of my life while maintaining the high quality and attention to detail I bring to my professional work.

## What You Can Expect

This site will evolve as I do, but here's what I'm planning:

### Technical Content
- **Deep dives** into data engineering challenges and solutions
- **System design** thoughts and patterns I've learned
- **Tool reviews** and technology explorations
- **Career insights** from my journey in tech

### Personal Content
- **Music discoveries** and playlist archaeology
- **Running adventures** and training insights
- **Coffee experiments** and brewing notes
- **Photography** projects and film developing adventures

### A Mix of Both
- **Book reviews** (both technical and fiction)
- **Travel stories** with a tech worker's perspective
- **Productivity systems** that actually work
- **Random thoughts** that don't fit neat categories

## The Technical Details

For those curious about the implementation, this site is built with:

- **Astro** for lightning-fast static generation
- **Tailwind CSS** for responsive, maintainable styling
- **MDX** for rich content authoring
- **TypeScript** for type safety and better development experience

It's hosted on GitHub Pages and designed to score 95+ on Lighthouse performance metrics because fast sites respect users' time and bandwidth.
